NFT AND ITS MARKET PLACE
The first product that will be unleashed into the manifolds ecosystem is the NFT marketplace.
This NFT marketplace of manifolds will involve two types of markets, the first will be a marketplace for native manifolds NTFs while the second will be the marketplace for secondary projects of manifolds partners or projects decided by the community.
Our native manifolds nfts which will be a total of 10000 copies, the first which will be a total of 5000 copies will be tradable and give buffers to the second types of manifold ntfs which form the core of manifold ecosystem.
The nature of the set is very unique because it will be the bedrock of the learn to earn product.
Our native manifolds NFTs are actually the ticket to access various upcoming products to be launched on the manifolds ecosystem, especially the learn to earn, decentralised commerce and streaming platform.
These first set of special NFTs once owned give access to the holders discount rate, free $MANI and special access to other upcoming platforms, in the long run more utility will be added to the native NFTs as decided by the community.

The second set of native NFTs won't be bought and owned by individuals or companies but by the manifolds corporation and will only be leased out based on agreement between manifold team and the individual interested after fulfilling some terms of agreement.
Manifolds will be the first to implement such which thereby reduces volatility in price of some native NFTs as all native NFTs will have the same leasing price based on product and level, also it won't be affected by demand of some native NFTs over the other.
Secondary market NFTs can be bought and sold in our marketplace based on community decisions and partnerships with other secondary market places and there are plans of creating cross chains with massive NFTs markets across well known blockchains.

DECENTRALISED COMMERCE
Many centralised commercial apps such as amazon, jumia, alibaba and others have over the time become inefficient especially in terms of data security, KYC of users and tracking of goods bought/sold through their platforms.
To solve the various inefficiencies surrounding centralised commerce especially in developing countries all over the world, Manifolds has decided to use blockchain as the foundation in building its own decentralised commercial platform.
The concept here is to use blockchain to keep users information, track goods bought/sold through the platform, enable actions when there is failure in delivery or payment of Goods and finally remove bottlenecks in our traditional commercial sectors.
This will enable individuals, companies and governments to keep track various commercial activities going on within a particular geographical area.
The team plan is to make the decentralised commerce platform partly centralised and bring in legal entities. Along the line as trust is being built by end users of this platform, regular well known traders with high trust scores will be given a verified ticker which will give them some decentralised benefits.

Another untapped benefit of blockchain is in the agricultural sector where there is always issues involving farmers lacking funds to do commercial
farming especially of cash crops, poor storage of farm yields and poor markets place for their farm yields.
Manifold plans to use the power of blockchain to provide solutions to the agricultural sector by creating a platform whereby farmers can seek for investment funds either by asking for loans or partnering with investors willing to give them the needed funds to make their farm lands productive.
Also Via this platform farmers can advertise their farm yields at various stages of development and get in touch with marketplaces, individuals and companies wishing to buy the yields of the farm at favourable prices.
The whole idea is to create a platform connecting farmers, investors and marketplaces where there is an envisioned plan to increase the use case of this platform as the community seem fit.
